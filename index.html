About Us

Grow your business with us

Global Engineering Technologies (GET) is a Chennai-based company with the background of 15 years of experience in providing tooling solutions for all industrial requirements. We are specialized in bending tools,punching tools, laser consumables, woodworking and metal cutting.

BENDING TOOLS

We are proud to be the direct dealer of Rolleri S.p.A. Italy, the world's leading company for distributing bending tools. Discover our top-quality bending solutions.

LASER CONSUMABLES

We are multi-brand sellers of laser consumables, offering a wide range of high-quality products.

PUNCHING TOOLS

We promote ROLLERI and KETEC punching tools, offering top-notch quality and precision. Explore our range to meet all your punching needs.

WOODWORKING TOOLS

We offer a variety of saw blades and other tools from the top brands of Italy. Discover the perfect tools for your cutting needs.

METAL CUTTING SAW BLADES

Our metal cutting saw blades are designed for superior performance and longevity. Choose us to achieve precise cuts every time.

GRINDING SERVICE

Our grinding service offers exceptional accuracy and quality. Trust us for all your grinding needs, ensuring optimal results and can be reground multiple times, providing extended usability.
