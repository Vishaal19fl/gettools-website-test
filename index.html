About Us

Grow your business with us

Established in 2022 by founder Sathya P, Global Engineering Technology (GET) is a Chennai-based company dedicated to providing engineering solutions for all industrial requirements, with a primary focus on sheet metal fabrications and woodworking. We specialise in offering punching, bending solutions, and pneumatics to meet diverse manufacturing needs.

At GET, customer satisfaction and the delivery of high-quality tools are at the core of our values. We take pride in being the Indian supplier of Rolleri, Italy, a prominent company renowned for press brake, punching, bending, and laser solutions.

In addition to our standard offerings, we excel in designing and manufacturing specific and special tools based on individual demands. Our commitment to excellence, paired with our ability to customise solutions, sets us apart in the industry.
